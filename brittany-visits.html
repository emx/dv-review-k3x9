Maharaj in Brittany

Shri Ranjit Maharaj came to the French Atlantic coast five times, every year from 1996 to 2000 — about 55 recorded days of talks in all. Each visit below carries its dates, one line of context, and the exchanges worth remembering.

Sources. Visit ranges come from the five finding-aid Tables of Contents (e.g. 1997-06-16-06-29-brittany-toc); every individual date was then confirmed by a header search of the transcripts, including four days a capped first search had hidden. “Dates” means dates with a transcript — he was presumably present on the three gap days inside visit ranges, but no talk was recorded.

Method & notes

Two-way verification. The visit ranges come from the finding-aid Tables of Contents and Indexes of Topics that accompany each Brittany folder in the archive; the per-day roster was then confirmed by searching each date's transcript header. Four days (2 Jun 1996, 17 Jun 1997, 26–27 Jun 1997) were initially hidden by a capped search and recovered by targeted checks — as was 16 Jun 2000.

Gap days. 1 June 1998 and 3 & 6 May 1999 fall inside visit ranges but have no transcript — rest days or unrecorded sessions, not absences.

One archive quirk. The 1998 TOC is titled “26th May to 5th June” but the folder is named …-06-06 and a 6 June transcript exists.

Going deeper. Each visit's finding aids (e.g. 1999-04-30-05-09-brittany-toc and …-topics) hold a per-day table of contents and topic index for the full retreat.
